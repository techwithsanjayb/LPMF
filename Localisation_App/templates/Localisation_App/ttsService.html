TTS

Text-to-Speech (TTS) is a software which renders the machine readable text into Human Voice. The TTS software allows people with visual impairments or reading disabilities to listen to written works on a computer or a mobile device. Text to Speech system when integrated with screen reader would enable visually challenged users to interpret and perform computer operations with audio interface.
